Porting passwordless to koa

Every now and then you bump into a project that is really cool but is very much incompatible with the framework of your choice. For me, https://passwordless.net/ is one of those projects. The project itself was created by https://twitter.com/thesumofall and primarily for https://expressjs.com/This project is also a couple years old and although it is perfect and works flawlessly with https://expressjs.com/, it is a prime candidate to practice my refactoring skills and all the stuff encouraged by the development community. So, we will be focusing on a test driven development approach.

Make it work. Make it right. Make it fast.

On top of the test driven development we will completely move toward es6 and async/await type syntax for the library to make it more aligned with the koa syntax.

Stick around for the next couple of posts.
